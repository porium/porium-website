Introduction to distributed ledgers

This module will introduce you to decentralization, what it is and the structures of distributed ledger technology

The Decentralized Web is a series of technologies that replace or augment current communication protocols, networks, and services and distribute them in a way that is robust against single-actor control or censorship. Simply put it’s an architectural system that builds services on the internet which do not depend on any single “central” organisation to function.

A History of Digital Money

This module will give you an in-depth view of how Bitcoin and blockchain technology conceptualized decentralized digital currency operations.

This course will trace the history of cryptocurrency from the initial proposals for digital currencies, pre-Bitcoin, the early history of Bitcoin, the increasing popularity of Alternative Coins (‘Altcoins’), Central Bank Digital Coins (CBDC) and the explosion in popularity of cryptocurrency in 2017.

The Components, Structure & Benefits

This module will learn the core components of blockchain architecture, key characteristics of Blockchain architecture, blockchain algorithm, and types of Blockchain architecture.

Blockchain architecture explains everything that is involved in the development of this technology, for example consensus algorithms. There also varying types of architectures including public and private blockchains. You will also learn the embedded characteristics of blockchain architecture that possess a lot of benefits for businesses.

Application of Blockchain Technology

In this module you will learn the major sectors presently blockchain solutions to address recurrent industry problems.

Be inspired by how innovators are transforming businesses through use cases built on various blockchain networks. Applications have been built to address critical challenges in industries like banking & finance, healthcare, supply chain, insurance and media entertainment.
